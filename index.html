Welcome to your Akan name generator

In Ghanaian culture, when a baby is born, they are given a cultural name based on the day they were born. These names give pride to a person and tether them to the community despite the ever changing world we live in.

If you are male, the following day names are applicable to you:

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

Whereas if you are female, the following names would be given to you:

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
